Sunday
Morning Café

Technology moves fast. Do we have to, too?

Every other Sunday, I share my reflections and insights how technology changes the way I think, learn and feel, in my Sunday Morning blog series, working through the questions that matter to me most.

How is AI actually reshaping work and learning? What does it mean for how we show up, and do we even get to choose?

How do we grow without burning out in an industry that never stops? What does meaningful learning look like when 'keeping up' feels impossible?

What does it mean to live intentionally when technology offers infinite possibilities, and infinite distractions? How do we make conscious choices instead of just reacting?

Want to know more?

Grab a coffee and join me at the Sunday Morning Café.
