Hi, I'm
Nicole.

I'm a writer and thinker based in Europe, exploring the space where technology meets human experience. Every other Sunday, I sit down with a coffee and write about the questions that won't leave me alone.

I've spent years working in the tech industry — close enough to see its promise, and close enough to notice what it quietly costs us. That tension is what drives the Sunday Morning Café: not a verdict on technology, but an honest conversation about it.

My writing explores AI & Technology, how we learn without burning out in Learning in Tech, and what it means to live with intention in Thoughtful Living. I don't have all the answers — but I believe the questions are worth sitting with.

If any of this resonates, I'd love to have you at the Café. Pull up a chair.
